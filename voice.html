What is
Voice
Service?

Voice Service allows you to automate and streamline your communication through pre-recorded voice messages. Whether it's delivering important announcements, conducting surveys, or sending personalized greetings, our Voice Service ensures that your messages are conveyed with clarity and impact

Features

Automated Call Campaigns

Send time-sensitive information instantly with our SMS service. Ensure your messages reach your audience at the right moment for maximum impact.

Personalized Voice Messages

Tailor your SMS content to create a personalized experience. Include recipient names, customize offers, and make your messages more engaging.

Interactive Voice Response (IVR)

Enable two-way communication by allowing recipients to respond to your messages. Facilitate feedback, inquiries, and customer engagement seamlessly.

Scheduled Voice Campaigns

Plan your campaigns in advance with our scheduled messaging feature. Set specific dates and times for your messages to be sent, optimizing your communication strategy.

Analytics and Reporting

Track the performance of your SMS campaigns with detailed analytics. Monitor delivery rates, open rates, and more to refine your strategy for better results.
